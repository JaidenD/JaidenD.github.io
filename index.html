Research

Currently, my research involves computing symmetries and conserved quantities of relativistic fluid dynamics. ' This is a particularly interesting problem because curved space-time requires working in a coordinate-free setting, which forces computations to be carried out by hand. Additionally, pressure functions and relativity individually yield rich symmetries. The hope is that by combining these properties, we will uncover very nice and unexpected symmetries.
